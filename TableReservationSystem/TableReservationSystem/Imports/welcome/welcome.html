About Us

With an inventive, extensive menu and a put-you-at-ease atmosphere, Café Java emerged in 2021 as a contemporary destination for the hungry and sleep-deprived, and we’ve been savoring the fun ever since. Since our creation we’ve had the continued desire to create a space where casual comfort welcomes a cultivated palate — your go-to place for a quick coffee, a leisurely brunch, or simply just because. Browse our menus, see what we’re up to in the community, and always feel free to let us know how we’re doing.
